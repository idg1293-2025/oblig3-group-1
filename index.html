Forests have a significant role in reducing the risk of natural disasters, including floods, droughts, landslides and other extreme events.

Investing in forests and forestry represents an investment in people and their livelihoods.

Forests are the most biologically-diverse ecosystems on land, home to more than 80% of terrestrial species.
